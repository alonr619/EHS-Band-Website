Eastlake High School Band

Band Camp

Late August

A week of introducing marching band to incoming freshmen and new band members! You'll learn how to march in time, and you'll get plenty of practice with our pep band repertoire. Sections will get to know each other and begin bonding as a community!

Football Games

Late September - Early November

During the fall, Eastlake's band plays at football games, performing "vamps" to get the crowd hyped up and support our football team, as well as running field shows to entertain during halftime. There are four of these games, along with a possibility of optional games if our team makes it to the playoffs!

Field Shows

During Football Game Halftimes

The most fun part of any football game! We practice our marching and music throughout the week during band class, sometimes with afterschool or wolf time rehearsals, then we perform it during halftime. We have four of these every year!
